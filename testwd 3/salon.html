The Salon

 

The salon at Furry Friends Training and Care, Offer complimentary* Grooming for Dog’s with us for 5 nights and over.

Grooming

Brush and comb through with light mat removal. Bath with shampoo tailored to your pet’s coat type and needs. Complementary Blueberry facial and massage. Ear clean. Nail trim (if tolerated) and file. Paw pad trim (if required). Sanitary areas light trim if required. Hypoallergenic skin and coat treatments for pets with sensitive skins and / or allergies. Plus our complementary, introductory offer dead sea mineral deep conditioning treatment for skin and coat with warm towel wrap. Fluff dry. And finished off with a complimentary doggy bow and spitz of doggy cologne.
